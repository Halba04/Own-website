How To Book Your Sessions

TO DO LIST

  • Press "Link to Book"
  • Check the calendly for availability
  • Select the time slot that works for you
  • Book this time slot
  • Once you do this you are all set !

What to Expect

  1. First Session get to know player.
  2. Work on the basics: Touching, Control, Dribbling.
  3. Work on agility
  4. Work on our receiving technique and how to have body set up for our next move.
  5. Work on proper shooting
  6. Continue to work on all of above until close to perfection.
